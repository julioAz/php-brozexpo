About Us

WE ARE a Brazilian company, based in the heart of the city of Rio de Janeiro, close to the port area.

It was created with the purpose of promoting the access of high quality Brazilian food products to the international market. One of its main characteristics is the attention to products developed by micro and small companies, the so-called MSEs. We are committed to our international customers to offer high quality products, which respect the best environmental, administrative and social practices.
